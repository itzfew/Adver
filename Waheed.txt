'''D K Pora''' Or '''Dreri Kali pora,''' is village of tehsil Herman and district shopian, jammu and kashmir, it has total population of 2242 as per census 2011<ref>{{Cite web |title=Dreri Kali Pora Village Population - Shopian - Shupiyan, Jammu and Kashmir |url=https://www.census2011.co.in/data/village/3410-dreri-kali-pora-jammu-and-kashmir.html |access-date=2025-02-01 |website=www.census2011.co.in}}</ref>, DK pora shopian has a two schools upto matrix named NOOR-UL-HUDA HANFIA INS.(Private)<ref>{{Cite web |title=NOOR-UL-HUDA HANFIA INS. - D.k.pora District Shopian (Jammu And Kashmir) |url=https://schools.org.in/shopian/01150203702/noor-ul-huda-hanfia-ins.html |access-date=2025-02-01 |website=schools.org.in}}</ref> and High School (government)<ref>{{Cite web |title=HIGH SCHOOL D.K.PORA - D.k.pora District Shopian (Jammu And Kashmir) |url=https://schools.org.in/shopian/01150203701/high-school-d-k-pora.html |access-date=2025-02-01 |website=schools.org.in}}</ref> and also has Primary Health Centre (Hospital)<ref>{{Cite web |last=Primary |first=Health Center |date=2018-09-14 |title=Primary Health Centre in Shopian Shines a Ray of Hope |url=https://kashmirunheard.org/2018/09/14/primary-health-centre-in-shopian-shines-a-ray-of-hope/ |access-date=2025-02-01 |website=Kashmir Unheard |language=en-US}}</ref>




==Agriculture==
[[Agriculture]] is the main occupation of Herman village. Apple growing is the main source of livelihood of majority of households.<ref>{{Cite book|last=Bhat|first=T.|date=2014|title=Economic of Apple Industry:A Primary Survey in District Shopian Kashmir (India)|s2cid=166638356 |language=en}}</ref>

==Financial institutions==
Banks and finance institutions has also established their branches in the village. In Hermain there is a branch of J&K Bank having an ATM.
Ellaqui Dehatti Bank Branch. A branch of central cooperative bank also is working there.<ref>{{cite news |title=IFSC Code For JAMMU AND KASHMIR BANK LIMITED SHOPIAN - JAMMU AND KASHMIR |url=https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/wealth/ifsccode/bank-jammu-and-kashmir-bank-limited,state-jammu-and-kashmir,district-shopian,branch-herman,ifsccode-JAKA0HERMAN.cms}}</ref><ref>{{cite news |title=JkPsc |url=http://www.jkpsc.nic.in/pdf/SELECTION_ARABIC_2017.pdf}}</ref>

==Population and geographical area==
According to the 2011 census, Herman has a total population of 3,334 peoples. There are about 513 houses in Herman village. In 2011, the literacy rate of Herman village was 62.60%. In Herman, Male literacy stands at 72.44% while female literacy rate was 53.66%.<ref>{{cite news |title=Herman Population - Shupiyan, Jammu and Kashmir |url=https://www.census2011.co.in/data/village/3448-herman-jammu-and-kashmir.html}}</ref>
